# SayCard Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-20

SayCard is a lightweight content creation app for captions, replies, and
shareable vibe cards.

SayCard V1 is designed to work without an account, without ads, without
tracking, and without a self-built server.

## Data We Do Not Collect

In the current V1 app, we do not collect personal data from SayCard.

We do not collect:

- Names
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Contacts
- Location
- Photos or videos
- Audio recordings
- Advertising identifiers
- Analytics events
- Generated prompts or results
- Saved history
- Beta activation counters

## Local App Data

Some data is stored locally on your device so the app can function:

- Generated results saved in local history
- Daily free generation quota
- Local beta signal counters shown in Settings
- Share Extension handoff data used to continue a shared item in the app

This data stays on your device in V1. We do not upload it to a SayCard server.

## AI Generation

When available, SayCard uses Apple Foundation Models on device.

On unsupported devices or unavailable system-model states, SayCard uses a local
fallback generator. SayCard V1 does not send your prompts or results to a
self-built cloud AI server.

## Purchases

SayCard Pro purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit and App Store purchase
infrastructure.

We do not collect or store your payment card, bank account, or billing
information.

## Tracking And Advertising

SayCard V1 does not use ads, ad attribution, analytics SDKs, App Tracking
Transparency, or tracking identifiers.

## Changes

If SayCard later adds accounts, cloud sync, analytics, any non-local
processing, customer support forms, or third-party SDKs, this policy and the App
Store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.
